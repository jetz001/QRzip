How It Works

Three steps to a compact QR.

From raw text to scannable QR in milliseconds — simple by design.

1
Paste or type your text
Drop in any text — a URL, JSON, a Thai message, or a developer token. No setup needed.
2
QRzip picks the best algorithm
Our engine benchmarks 9 lossless algorithms and auto-selects the one that produces the smallest encoded payload for your specific text.

Get a compact, scannable QR
